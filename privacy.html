Privacy Policy

Effective date: 06/01/2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Scent Lab ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when using our personal automation workflow built in n8n to publish content to social platforms (including TikTok) for our own accounts.

1) What this automation does

The automation retrieves video files from cloud storage (e.g., Google Drive), generates captions/metadata using an AI model, and posts videos/captions to our social accounts using official platform APIs.

2) Information we collect

  • OAuth tokens and identifiers (e.g., TikTok access_token, refresh_token, open_id) required to authenticate API requests.
  • Content metadata you provide or we generate (titles, captions, hashtags, tags, category selections).
  • Video files and media that are processed solely for upload/posting.
  • Operational logs produced by n8n for debugging (timestamps, node execution status, error messages).

3) How we use the information

  • To authenticate and interact with platform APIs (e.g., upload and publish content).
  • To generate and attach captions/metadata to posts.
  • To troubleshoot failures, monitor automation runs, and improve reliability.

4) Sharing of information

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only with:

  • TikTok and other connected platforms, as required to upload/publish content.
  • Service providers you connect (e.g., Google services) strictly to retrieve media files.
  • Infrastructure providers hosting our n8n instance (if applicable).

5) Storage, security, and access

Tokens and configuration values are stored in the n8n instance credentials/storage. We take reasonable technical measures to protect data (access controls, secure passwords, HTTPS where available). No method of storage is 100% secure.

6) Data retention

  • OAuth tokens: kept while needed for automation and refreshed as required.
  • Logs: retained for operational debugging and may be deleted periodically.
  • Media files: processed for upload; not intentionally stored beyond workflow needs unless your storage provider retains them.

7) Your choices and rights

You can revoke access at any time by removing the app authorization in your TikTok account settings (or other platform settings). You may also request deletion of stored tokens/logs by contacting us.
